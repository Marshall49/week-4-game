Team Collectors

- You will be given a random number at the start of the game

- There are four Teams below. By clicking on a Team you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

- You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

- The value of each Team is hidden from you until you click on it.

- Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of each Team.
